How to cook a simple Sisig
Sisig is a Filipino dish made from parts of a pig's face and belly and chicken liver
  • 500g. pig's mask or belly
  • as needed canola oil
  • 1 pc red onion, chopped
  • 1 (1-inch)pc gringer, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chicken liver, chopped(optional)
  • 1pc green chilli, sliced
  • as needed bird's eye chilli, sliced
  • as needed knorr liquid seosoning
  • 5 tbsps lady's Choice real mayonnaise
  • 3 pcs calamansi
